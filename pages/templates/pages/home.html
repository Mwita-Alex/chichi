Stakeholders roundtable meetings

Bungoma County Stakeholders Roundtable Meeting Earlier today UCSPAK held a constructive engagement with key partners in Bungoma County on how to Increase meaningful youth participation in peace and governance (electoral) processes. The Stakeholders meeting brought together various actors including the Bungoma County Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission(IEBC) officials, a representative from the National Cohesion and Integration Commission; Assistant Dean of Students from Kibabi University, and various student leaders. It's indeed great to see young people in institution of learning taking lead in peace building and good governance
