The Secretariat are full time employees of the Association. They are in charge of the day-to-day affairs of the Association. The Secretariat is headed by the Executive Director who is assisted by two Deputies (the deputy executive director, Finance and the Deputy Executive Director, Programs). The deputies are further assisted by senior manager and officers. Below are current staff for the Association.
