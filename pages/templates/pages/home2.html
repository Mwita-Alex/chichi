We would like to thank all the partners who participated in the validation workshop for our research on Youth and Peacebuilding at Sarova Panafric today. Present was the Ministry of Interior, Ministry of public service, Youth and Gender, UNDP, NCIC, among others. Our special appreciation goes to the National Steering Committee on Peace Building and Conflict Management (NSC) and UNDP for supporting us in this research . The purpose of the research was to provide a baseline information and analysis defining a strategy for the development of the Kenya National Action Plan on implementation of UNSCR 2250.
